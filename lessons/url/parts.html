This is a URL. You type it into an address bar to request content from the internet. URLs serve as an address to web based content. URLs are made up of parts separated by /'s. Much like an address on an envelope the different parts of a URL provide your browser with contextual information regarding the whereabouts of whatever it is you are trying to access.
