The scheme is the metaphorical envelope from the parts.html page.

The scheme tells your browser which type of information it should expect to receive (much like an envelope typically means you will have a letter). For our purposes we will only need to know HTTP and HTTPS. HTTP is the primary language of the internet and the language your browser expects in order to draw a web page onto your screen. These acronyms stand for Hyper Text Transfer Protocol and Hyper Text Transfer Protocol Secure.
